IDEATHON 2022

THEMES

  1. Industrial Engineering and renewable energy resources
  2. Artificial intelligence and biotechnology
  3. Virtual Reality
  4. EdTech
  5. Blockchaining and Cybersecurity
  6. Smart vehicles/ motor and battery technology

RULES

  1. Students can participate individually or in a team of 2-4 Members.
  2. The registration fees for DIT students is Rs. 40/head and for Non-DIT students is Rs.70/head.
  3. The theme selected at the time of registration must not be changed.
  4. Each participant or team has to present an abstract of their ideas on any one of the given themes. In the abstract, only the important points are to be highlighted.The slides of presentation should not exceed 10 pages.
  5. Each report to include the innovations implemented, the monitoring mechanism indicating the impact of the innovation, challenges faced, the solutions developed to overcome these challenges, use of resources and funds generated as well as the learning from the project.
  6. Each team must share future steps, list reasons if project was partially completed.
  7. Presentation can include- videos, photographs and audio clips embedded in the presentation.
  8. If the abstract does not conform to the guidelines, it will not be screened.
  9. The ideas should be original. If any stage of competition, plagiarism is discovered by the judges, the participant shall be disqualified and next participant shall be offered a chance.
  10. The time for presentation of your idea should not exceed 5 minutes. A warning buzzer will be issued at 3 mins and 5 mins respectively. Teams taking extra time will lose points.
  11. Each team will present to a panel of esteemed judges, who will have the right to question the teams on their projects.
  12. The decision of the judges shall be final.
  13. In case of No-show, registration fees will not be refunded.
  14. Certificate of Participation will be given to all participating team members who showed up.

EVALUATION CRITERIA

  1. Originality of thought and content
  2. Creativity
  3. Logical Presentation, clarity of thought and idea
  4. Depth of Research
  5. Ability of the participant to build a startup with the proposed idea
